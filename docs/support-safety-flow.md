# Support And Safety Flow

Time trading needs trust from day one. The prototype separates ordinary support from safety escalation.

## Ordinary Support

- Scheduling confusion.
- Cancellation questions.
- Ledger hold questions.
- Low-quality session reports.

## Dispute Flow

1. User opens a dispute.
2. Related credits move to disputed state.
3. Support agent summarizes evidence.
4. Governance checks whether policy is clear.
5. Human reviewer decides release, partial release, or settlement.
6. Decision is logged.

## Safety Escalation

Safety issues include harassment, threats, coercion, identity fraud, or requests that could harm a user. Agents may summarize and route these issues, but they must not make final enforcement decisions without human approval.
